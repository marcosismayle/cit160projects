PROPERTY REGULATION

Many people didn't have the tradition to hire a professional to build their houses and don't have money enough to
buy one. So they start building step by step,even without a project, and before the conclusion, they are already
living there. When they got sick, theysearch for a doctor. But when they needs to construct, anyone can do it.
And there are many irregular buldings.

Our purpose is to provide a solution for irregular properties in order to provide all the documentation that the
legislation requires and adapt the building to current legislation. And also carry out all the necessary
procedures to regularize the start of a new venture.
